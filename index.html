Welcome to DenKim Hotel

Your perfect getaway starts here.

The DenKim Hotel is a classic treasure, set in cosy surroundings, elegantly designed to ease the pressures of life and offer ultimate comfort. The exquisite wooden finishing, marble floors and pure white walls encompass the elegant design that reveals the classic treasure that we are. The spacious, airy lobby, comfortable stylish seats, magnificent flowers, sophisticated style and luxurious finishes will definitely make your stay memorable. Our hotel encompasses 116 guest rooms, 14 meeting spaces that are the ideal spaces for your meetings, parties, trainings, dinners, launches and weddings, 3 Dining restaurants, Garden and the Health club wing (gym, spa, aerobics studio, spacious steam and sauna with relaxation rooms.) All our meeting spaces are fitted with a PA system and LCD Screens. Our largest ballroom Lenana Ballroom, is pillarless for uninterrupted views during your function. It can hold a capacity of up to 800 people. We undertake outside catering from 25 pax to as many as 5,000 pax or more for events in Nairobi and beyond, be it for office parties, business meetings, corporate and social events. Our seasoned chefs and banqueting team are specially trained to meet all your needs.
